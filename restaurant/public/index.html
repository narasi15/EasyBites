Who we are

This project was put together by two students at the University of Toronto, for the CSC309 course. If you see any of them on campus, feel free to offer some coffee, we can ALWAYS use some!

This program was created using tools like BootStrap to design the HTML and CSS files. The Zomato API was used to retrieve data about cities and places to eat.

Creators: Thanin Chan, Priyanka Narasimhan

Order

Don't wait for the waiter to bring you a menu. Search for your restaurant, place your order, pay securely online and receive your confirmation and service time.


Sit

When you arrive. There's no long lines or waiting list to sign. Just provide your confirmation information and you'll be seated immediately.


Eat

The restaurants start preparing your meal immediately after you order so when you get to your table, your food is hot and ready. No waiting!

Search

    Make a search and choose a restaurant. You must sign up to be able to perform a full search, else you will be limited to three cities. You will be able to view the menu and pricings to your order. You can also select whether you want to dine, order takeout and your pickup time.

Payment

    Quick and easy payment through online! Remember, you will also be charged if you make an order and do not show up within your suggested time frame!
